Case Studies This Week

Cane Toads (Australia)

Introduced 1935 to control beetles. Now 200M+ and killing native predators with their poison.

Zebra Mussels (Great Lakes)

Filter-feeders that outcompete native species. 150,000 per m² in some areas!

Kudzu (Southern US)

"The vine that ate the South" - grows 1 foot/day, smothers native trees.

Brown Tree Snake (Guam)

Caused extinction of 10/12 native bird species. Spider population exploded 40×!

Key Vocabulary

Invasive Species Non-native that outcompetes natives
Trophic Cascade Effects rippling through food web
Keystone Species Outsized ecosystem effects
Ecological Niche Species' role in ecosystem
Top-Down Cascade Predator changes affect all below
Bottom-Up Cascade Producer changes affect all above
Biocontrol Using organisms to control pests
Ecosystem Resilience Ability to recover from disruption
