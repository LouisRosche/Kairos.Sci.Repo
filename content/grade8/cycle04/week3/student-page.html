Week 1: Energy Pyramids

  • 10% Rule: Only 10% transfers between levels
  • 90% Lost: Heat, waste, metabolism, parts not eaten
  • Pyramid Shape: Less energy = fewer organisms at top
  • Biomass: Decreases at higher levels
  • Efficiency: Eating plants > eating animals

Week 2: Ecosystem Disruption

  • Invasive: No predators, outcompete natives
  • Trophic Cascade: Effects ripple through all levels
  • Keystone Species: Outsized ecosystem effects
  • Top-Down: Predator changes affect prey & below
  • Island Species: Lack evolved defenses
