Review Before You Begin

Week 1: Energy Pyramids

  • 10% rule: Only 10% transfers between levels
  • 90% lost to heat, waste, metabolism, parts not eaten
  • Pyramid shape: Less energy = fewer organisms at top
  • Biomass decreases at higher levels
  • Eating plants is more efficient than eating animals

Week 2: Ecosystem Disruption

  • Invasive species: No predators, outcompete natives
  • Trophic cascade: Effects ripple through all levels
  • Keystone species: Outsized effects on ecosystem
  • Top-down cascade: Predator changes affect prey and below
  • Island species: Vulnerable due to lack of evolved defenses
